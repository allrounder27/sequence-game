How to Play Sequence

🎯 Objective

Be the first player to complete 2 sequences — a sequence is 5 chips in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

🃏 Gameplay

  1. On your turn, click a card from your hand to select it.
  2. Then click a matching highlighted space on the board to place your chip.
  3. A new card is automatically drawn from the deck.
  4. Turn passes to the other player.

🃏 Special Jacks

WILD Two-Eyed Jacks (J♦, J♣): Place your chip on any empty space.

REMOVE One-Eyed Jacks (J♥, J♠): Remove one of your opponent's chips (not from a completed sequence).

⭐ Corner Spaces

The four corner ★ spaces are free for everyone — they count toward either player's sequence.

💀 Dead Cards

If both board spaces matching a card are already occupied, that card is dead. Click it to exchange for a new card (doesn't use your turn).
