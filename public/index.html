How to Play Sequence

🎯

Objective

Be the first team to complete 2 sequences — 5 chips in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

🎮

Gameplay

  1. Select a card from your hand
  2. Place your team's chip on a matching board space
  3. A new card is drawn & turn passes automatically
👥

Teams

2 players: 1v1 (7 cards) · 3 players: FFA (6 cards) · 4 players: 2v2 (6 cards) · 6 players: 2v2v2 (5 cards)

🃏

Special Jacks

WILD J♦ / J♣ — Place on any empty space
REMOVE J♥ / J♠ — Remove an opponent's chip
⭐

Free Corners

The 4 corner ★ spaces count as free for all teams.

💀

Dead Cards

If both spaces for a card are occupied, click it to exchange for a new card (free action, no turn cost).
